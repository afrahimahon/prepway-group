Vision

Built for
Impact, Not
Just Content.

Prepway Group was not built to produce videos. It was built to change outcomes — to give every Bangladeshi student access to guidance that was once available only to a privileged few.

We work across two distinct fronts: domestic education from SSC to university admission, and global pathways for Bangladeshi students pursuing opportunities abroad.

01

Domestic Excellence

SSC, HSC, medical admission, university prep — built in Bangla, for Bangladesh, by people who lived it.

02

Global Access

Scholarships, visas, applications, IELTS — helping Bangladeshi students reach universities worldwide.

03

Authentic Storytelling

Real students, unscripted conversations, honest journeys — no manufactured inspiration.

Our Mission

"From Bangladesh, to the world — and back again."

We exist to close the guidance gap — to make sure that no student in Bangladesh has to navigate their most important decisions alone. Whether the goal is a merit list at BUET or an admission letter from a university abroad, Prepway Group is building the resources to get them there.
